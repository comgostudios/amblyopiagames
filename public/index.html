How it works

1

Track your treatment plan

Keep all therapy activity in one place:

  • Patching time tracking
  • Daily reminders
  • Session history
  • Progress over time
  • Treatment streaks

Stay consistent and see how your therapy habits evolve.

2

Train using interactive exercises

Engaging browser-based visual activities designed to support binocular stimulation and visual training. Exercises may include:

  • Dichoptic exercises using red-cyan glasses
  • Visual attention activities
  • Contrast-based tasks
  • Adaptive difficulty levels

No app installs required. Start directly in your browser.

3

Share progress with your clinician

Patients and clinicians can work together using shared dashboards. Clinicians can:

  • Monitor patient adherence
  • Review therapy activity
  • Track session trends
  • Adjust recommendations
